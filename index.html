Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about working with GANA.AI LABS

What exactly is a diagnostic session?

It's a 30-minute conversation where we learn about your team, your challenges, and your goals. We'll identify 2-3 specific areas where AI or automation could help, and give you actionable recommendations — whether you work with us or not.

Do we need technical expertise to work with you?

Not at all. We work with teams of all technical levels. We explain everything in plain language and train your team to use the systems we build.

How long does implementation take?

It depends on the scope, but most projects take 2-6 weeks from start to finish. We work in phases so you see value quickly, not months later.

What if we're not sure AI is right for us?

That's exactly what the diagnostic session is for. We'll be honest about whether AI makes sense for your situation. Sometimes the answer is "not yet" — and that's okay.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. We offer maintenance packages and can provide ongoing support as your needs evolve. But we also design systems that your team can manage independently.

What makes GANA.AI LABS different from other AI consultants?

We focus on practical implementation, not impressive demos. We care about impact, not just technology. And we're committed to making AI accessible to organizations of all sizes, especially those working on social good.
